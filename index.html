About CrocoDuck

Our Story

CrocoDuck was founded in 2019 by two passionate parents, Emily and Michael, who were constantly on the hunt for new and engaging toys to spark their two children's curiosity and imagination. Frustrated by the lack of variety in toy stores and the hassle of constantly shopping for new toys, they decided to create a subscription service that would bring the joy and wonder of discovery right to families' doorsteps.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire creativity, learning, and endless fun for children aged 0-10 years old. We carefully curate each box with a thoughtful selection of high-quality, educational, and age-appropriate toys that encourage exploration, problem-solving, and imaginative play.

Our Promise

We understand the importance of providing children with toys that not only entertain but are also safe, fostering their cognitive, social, and emotional development. That's why our team of experts meticulously handpicks each item, ensuring that every toy in our boxes meets the highest standards of safety, durability, and educational value.

Our Process

Each month, our team of toy enthusiasts scours the market for the latest and greatest toys, seeking out unique, innovative, and engaging products that will captivate and delight children. We then carefully curate a collection of toys that complement each other, creating a well-rounded and stimulating play experience for both child and parent.

The Timeline for Boxes

  • 1st-5th day of the month: You will receive your subscription box!
  • 10th-19th day of the month: You will get an email about the upcoming subscription box toy and book lineup. During this time, you can select the items you think will interest your child(ren) the most and update your box. Or, you can trust our expert selections and make no changes. Either way, your little one(s) will receive items that they will love!
  • 20th-23rd day of the month: We will ship your subscription box. Expect to get tracking info sent to your email.
  • 24th-30th day of the month: Your subscription box will be moving through our courier network and be on the way to you.

*Note: You must choose each play toy by the 19th day of each month if you are not using our expert selections. An email reminder will be sent to the email address on file on the 10th and 15th day (if your selection has not been recorded) of each month.
If you are using our expert selections, just sit back and let us handle the rest!
